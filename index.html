About me

I am Jiwei Shen, a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science at East China Normal University. I have extensive experience leading and collaborating on AI research projects, including work on large language models, embodied intelligence, and applications in integrated circuit manufacturing. I have independently led multiple funded projects, published in top journals and conferences, and successfully mentored students who have won national innovation awards.
